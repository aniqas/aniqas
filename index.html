DOG BREEDS

Visual Name Description
Chihuahuas Chihuahuas' are the smallest dog breeds in the world. Though they are the smallest, the chihuahuas' are one of the breeds with longest lifespan with 18 years. They are perceived as the "luxurious" pets. However, they are also highly intelligent and very loyal to their owners.
Pomeranians They are small breeds but are considered to be cocky and highly intelligent. They are obedient small dogs who can master tricks very lively. Other than that, they are also bold and lively.
Pugs Pugs are one of the oldest dog breeds. They are another small breed. Though they are small, they are also playful and mischievous. Pugs are great family dogs since they are very playful and outgoing.
